ABOUT THE DUDE

STEVEN PELLETIER

DESIGNER

I was born in the surf town Oceanside, California. After moving to Cocoa, Florida in 2003, I graduated from Space Coast Jr/Sr High School in 2008.  Then I studied Journalism at the University of South Florida before transferring to the University of Central Florida to study Web Design.

 

I started my way making signatures in graphic forums and then experimented with Photoshop, Cinema4D, Maya, and other design programs. I knew how to code somewhat through high school but didn't realize I loved it until college. I am a graphic designer who loves to create websites!

MY GOALS

Right now, I am a server at Gator's Dockside working my way through college. I plan on getting an internship soon to vastly improve my skills while getting myself out there. I want to produce well designed websites that represent how far we have come in Digital Media. The University of Central Florida has facilities, faculty, and tools students need to succeed in the field.

Being one of the many Web Design majors at the school, The goal of this website specifically is to display my work in hopes of someone discovering the skills I possess to eventually lead me down a path to being a successful and amazing Web Designer.
